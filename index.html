Why EDA Is Important Before Model Training

Understand Data Quality

Detect missing values, outliers, duplicates and inconsistencies before training any model.

Prevent Garbage In → Garbage Out

Machine learning models amplify the quality of your data — good or bad.

Reveal Feature Relationships

Correlations and distributions guide smarter feature selection.

Choose the Right Model

EDA helps determine whether your task is classification, regression, clustering or time series.

What V1 Does

Data Summary

Automatically generates statistical summaries including mean, median, null values, and distributions.
V1 gives a quick snapshot of the data’s distribution, central tendencies, and missing values, along with visual insights.

Visual Insights

Creates histograms, box plots, correlation heatmaps, and feature comparisons instantly.
Categorical column insights (like bar charts for categories) are not generated in V1.
The report preview only shows first 20 rows; plots are based on the full dataset.

Auto imputation

Any NaN or empty cells in numeric columns, Any missing categorical values are replaced with the mean and mode of that column.
Even if your Excel file stores numbers as text, the tool converts them to numeric first
